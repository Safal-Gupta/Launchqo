Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about how Launchqo supports your startup journey

What kind of startups does Launchqo support?

Launchqo supports all types of startups — from tech and services to local and rural ideas. Whether you're building an app, a D2C brand, or just validating an idea, Launchqo offers resources and tools to help you get started.

Do I need any prior business experience to use Launchqo?

Not at all! Launchqo is designed especially for first-time founders and idea-stage entrepreneurs. Our platform walks you through each stage — from idea to launch — with guidance tailored for beginners.

What’s the difference between the Basic and Premium plans?

The Basic Plan includes essential tools like founder match, legal templates, and pitch builders. The Premium Plan offers full access — including one-on-one mentoring, funding support tools, and analytics to track your startup growth.

Can I upgrade later if I start with the Free plan?

Yes, absolutely. You can start with the Free plan and upgrade to Basic or Premium anytime during your 3-year journey to unlock more features and personalized tools.

How does the “Founder Match” feature work?

Founder Match uses your goals, skills, and preferences to connect you with like-minded co-founders, mentors, or collaborators — especially helpful if you’re building solo or seeking a team.
